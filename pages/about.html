About Me

I am from Winnetka, Illinois which is about 30 minutes north of Chicago (when there's not horrific traffic). I love all things Chicago -- deep-dish pizza, the Cubs, and Lake Michigan. I cannot wait to live in the city with my high school friends after graduation. I am the middle child of 3 siblings. My sister, Emily, is 28 and lives in Chicago with her husband, Michael. My brother, David, is 19 and is a freshman at Indiana University. My parents are Julie and Dave and they're the primary caretakers of my amazing Australian Shepherd, Cody. My hobbies include running with/walking with my dog, listening to music and going to concerts, and exploring new restaurants.

Chicago has been my home forever. I love the city and I can't wait to join my sister who is living there now.

My family is actually split between being fans of the Cubs or The White Sox. I personally root for the Cubs and love going to baseball games in the summer.

A top priority I have had when going home for breaks from Michigan has been immediately ordering a deep-dish pizza from Lou Malnati's or Giordano's.
